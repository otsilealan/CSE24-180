Travel Planning

Best Time to Visit

The dry season (May to October) offers the best wildlife viewing as animals gather around water sources. The green season (November to April) brings lush landscapes and newborn wildlife.

Quick Travel Tips

  • Safari vehicles are open-sided; pack accordingly
  • Bring binoculars for wildlife viewing
  • Malaria precautions recommended
  • Book accommodations well in advance
